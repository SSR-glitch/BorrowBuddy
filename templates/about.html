About BorrowBuddy

Your Friendly College Item Sharing Portal

Our Mission

BorrowBuddy with a simple mission: to make student life more affordable and sustainable. We noticed that students often buy items they only need for a short time, like a specific textbook, a calculator for an exam, or a piece of sports equipment. Meanwhile, other students have these exact items sitting unused.


How We Help

Our platform connects these students. By creating a trusted community for peer-to-peer lending, we help students save money, reduce waste, and get access to the things they need, right when they need them. Whether you're looking to borrow or lend, BorrowBuddy makes it easy and safe to share within our college community.


Join Our Community

We are a community built on trust and sharing. We believe that by helping each other, we can build a better, more resourceful campus environment for everyone.
