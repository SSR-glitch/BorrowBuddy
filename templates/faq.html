Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We're here to help.

How does BorrowBuddy work?

BorrowBuddy is a peer-to-peer platform where students can list items for others to rent. You can browse for items, pay the rental fee to send a request to the owner, and arrange a pickup if the owner approves your request.

Are there fees to use the service?

Listing an item is completely free for lenders. For borrowers, there is a rental fee for each item, which is set by the item's owner. This fee must be paid before your rental request is sent. Some items may also require an additional refundable security deposit.

What is the difference between a Rental Fee and a Security Deposit?

The Rental Fee is the cost to rent the item. This is a non-refundable payment that goes to the item's owner. The Security Deposit is an extra, optional amount held for security. This amount is fully refunded to you after you return the item safely.

What happens if an item is returned late or damaged?

The security deposit is held to cover such situations. If there's an issue, both the lender and borrower should first try to resolve it themselves. If they can't, they can contact our support team to mediate. Please refer to our Terms of Service for the full policy.

How do I get my security deposit back?

Once the lender confirms that the item has been returned safely, the security deposit is automatically refunded to your original payment method. The refund process typically takes 5-7 business days.
